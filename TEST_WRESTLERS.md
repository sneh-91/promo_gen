# Test Wrestlers

Two contrasting templates for quickly populating the wrestler form. Copy each
field directly into its matching input. Try **Wrestler 1 first on the mic** for
a "this is my house" babyface opener, or **Wrestler 2 first** for a smug heel
opener — both flows work and produce different feels.

---

## Wrestler 1 — "Diesel" Dorian Vance

**Name**

```
"Diesel" Dorian Vance
```

**Alignment**: `babyface`

**Size**: `big`

**Look**

```
6'5", 270 lbs, broad-shouldered ex-college football player build. Black short-cropped hair, neatly trimmed beard, a faded scar along his left cheekbone. Wears red-and-black gear with hand-taped boxer wraps and a small steel cross on a leather cord around his neck. Steel-blue eyes, easy half-smile that drops the moment the bell rings. Walks to the ring slow, like he's already done this a thousand times.
```

**Description**

```
Former offensive lineman who washed out of the NFL after a torn ACL in his second season, then spent six years grinding the indie circuit — sleeping in his truck between shows, working construction during the day — before finally getting called up. Built his name on toughness, never quitting, and treating fans like family. Quiet by nature, but when he talks he means it. Carries a chip on his shoulder about being told for years he wasn't "marketable enough." Believes in earning everything the hard way and has a soft spot for blue-collar crowds. Hates flashy guys who haven't paid dues. His finisher is a sit-out spinebuster called the "Hard Hat."
```

## Wrestler 2 — Kage Sterling

**Name**

```
Kage Sterling
```

**Alignment**: `heel`

**Size**: `average`

**Look**

```
6'1", 215 lbs, lean and wiry, model-handsome in a calculated way. Slicked-back jet-black hair, sharp jawline, a permanent smirk like he just heard a joke nobody else got. Tailored black-and-gold gear with custom embroidery on the trunks, an embroidered designer ring jacket he keeps on as long as possible, and expensive aviator sunglasses he refuses to take off until the bell. Diamond earrings in both ears, pale skin, cold green eyes. Walks down the ramp like the entire arena is his foyer.
```

**Description**

```
Old-money trust-fund kid whose family bought his way into three of the most expensive wrestling academies in the world. Flopped out of two indie promotions before a phone call from his father landed him a developmental contract. Genuinely talented in the ring — clean technical wrestler, fast hands, sharp counters — but never had to bleed for any of it, and opponents can feel the hollowness in his work. Treats fans as "the help" and refers to his locker room as "the staff." References his net worth, his cars, and his lineage in every promo. Hates being called soft, hates being told he hasn't earned it, and absolutely cannot stand being made to look poor. His finisher is a vicious snapping cutter he calls the "Family Inheritance."
```

---

## Why this pairing works

- **"Earned vs. bought"** gives both wrestlers very specific things to attack.
- Diesel's `look` (working-class, scarred, taped wraps) gives Kage easy mockery.
- Kage's wealth, sunglasses, "the help" attitude give Diesel righteous-fury fuel.
- The size mismatch (`big` vs. `average`) gives the model a physical hook.
- Both have specific finisher names and backstory hooks the model calls out by
  name in promos.
